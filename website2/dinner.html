Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food made with a spiced vegetable mash (bhaji) served with buttered pav (bread rolls). Here's a simple recipe to make Pav Bhaji at home: 
                    Ingredients:
                    For the Bhaji:
                    2 medium-sized potatoes, boiled and mashed
                    1 cup cauliflower, chopped
                    1/2 cup green peas
                    1 onion, finely chopped
                    1 bell pepper (capsicum), finely chopped
                    2 tomatoes, finely chopped
                    1-2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)
                    1-2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
                    1 teaspoon turmeric powder
                    2 teaspoons red chili powder
                    1 tablespoon pav bhaji masala
                    Salt to taste
                    2 tablespoons butter
                    1 tablespoon oil
                    1 tablespoon lemon juice
                    Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
                    For the Pav (bread rolls):
                    6 pav buns
                    2 tablespoons butter
                    Enjoy your delicious Pav Bhaji!
